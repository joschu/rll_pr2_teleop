
Press LT and wave Left Paddle to move left gripper.
Press RT and wave Right Paddle to move right gripper.
orientation of gripper is absolute. 
translation of gripper is relative to the location of trigger press.

Pressing 1 on each paddle will close respective gripper
Pressing 2 on each paddle will open respective gripper

Right Joystick is used for base translation
Left Joystick is used for base rotation (might be inverted)

Hold center unmarked button on left paddle to change right joystick into head control.
Y-axis for head control is normal (not inverted)
